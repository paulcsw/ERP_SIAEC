{# 1. How to update tasks #}
How to Update Tasks

1. Go to Tasks tab and select an aircraft.

2. Tap on a task card to open Quick Update.

3. Update the status using the 4-button grid.

4. Adjust Man-Hours using the +/- stepper (cumulative).

5. Tap Save to update, or Save & Next to move to the next task.

{# 2. How to submit OT #}
How to Submit OT

1. Go to OT tab → Submit segment.

2. Fill in date, start/end time, reason, and optional details.

3. Check your monthly OT usage at the top (max 72h/month).

4. Tap Submit. Your supervisor will be notified for endorsement.

5. After endorsement, an admin will give final approval.

{# 3. Save failure troubleshooting #}
When Save Fails

Version Conflict: Another user modified the same task. Refresh and try again.

MH Decrease Forbidden: MH is cumulative and cannot be lowered. Contact your admin if a correction is needed.

Network Error: Check your internet connection and try again. Your input is preserved.

Permission Denied: You may not have edit access to this shop. Contact your admin.
