Help

How to update tasks

Tap an aircraft → select a task → update status and man-hours → tap Save. Use "Save & Next" to move to the next task automatically.

How to submit OT

Go to OT tab → fill in date, time, and reason → tap Submit. Your supervisor will review first, then admin gives final approval.

Save not working?

If you see "Updated by another user", tap "Reload latest" to get the current version. Your remarks can be copied. Check your internet connection if save keeps failing.
